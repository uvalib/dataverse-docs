Dataverse Collection Management
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

A dataverse collection is a container for datasets (research data and other products of research, code, documentation, and metadata) and other Dataverse collections, 
which can be setup for individual researchers, departments, journals and organizations.

Request to create a dataverse collection
===========================================
Submit a request to create a dataverse collection for your lab, department or center . Please email libra@virginia.edu.
