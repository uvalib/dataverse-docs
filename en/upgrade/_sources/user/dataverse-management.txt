Dataverse Management
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

A dataverse is a container for datasets (research data and other products of research, code, documentation, and metadata) and other dataverses, 
which can be setup for individual researchers, departments, journals and organizations.

|image1|


Request to create a dataverse
===============================
LibraData users must submit a request to create a dataverse.

If your department, lab, or center would like a dataverse added, please email libra@virginia.edu.
